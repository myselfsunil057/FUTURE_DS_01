# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard

## 🧠 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Sales Performance Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze revenue, profit, product performance, and business trends across time, categories, regions, and customer segments.

The goal is to transform raw sales data into **actionable business insights** for strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze overall business performance (Revenue, Profit, Margin)
- Identify top-performing and underperforming products/categories
- Understand sales and profit trends over time
- Evaluate regional and customer segment performance
- Support data-driven business decisions through visualization

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- 💰 Total Revenue: $2M  
- 📈 Net Profit: $286K  
- 📦 Total Orders: 5009  
- 📊 Profit Margin: 12.47%  

---

## 📌 Dashboard Features

### 🧾 KPI Summary
- Revenue, Profit, Order Volume, Profit Margin
- Clean card-based layout with icons
- High-level business snapshot

### 📦 Category Analysis
- Revenue contribution by product categories
- Identification of top and low-performing segments

### 🧩 Sub-Category Insights
- Distribution across Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies
- Performance comparison across business units

### 🏆 Top Products Analysis
- Top 10 revenue-generating products
- Highlights product-level dependency patterns

### 📈 Trend Analysis
- Sales and Profit trends (2014–2017)
- Business growth trajectory over time

### 🌍 Filters & Slicers
- Region-wise analysis (Central, East, South, West)
- Customer Segments (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
- Year-based filtering for dynamic insights

---

## 📊 Key Business Insights

- Revenue is strong but profit margin is **moderate (~12.5%)**
- High dependency on **Phones and Chairs for revenue generation**
- One product significantly outperforms others (concentration risk)
- Business shows **steady recovery and growth post-2015**
- Technology segment leads overall contribution
- Portfolio is diversified but uneven in performance distribution

---

## ⚠️ Business Risks Identified
- Revenue concentration in few categories/products
- Moderate profit efficiency
- Uneven product performance across portfolio

---

## 🚀 Business Opportunities
- Optimize pricing and cost structure to improve margins
- Scale Technology segment for higher ROI
- Strengthen mid-performing product categories
- Replicate success patterns of top-performing products
